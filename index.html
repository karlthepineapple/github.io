Illinois Online Voter Registration

Redesign of the Illinois online voter registration is the result of a rapid design challenge. Guided and dedicated time to put UI/UX skills to the test with small and focused projects with a peer critique for continued interations. With only four house to build a profile, story, and create a design.

Potential use cases:
  • An Illinois resident wants to complete a voter registration application
  • An Illinois resident wants to know the qualifications to register
  • An Illinois resident wants to find out if they are already registered
  • An Illinois resident want to know the status of their application
Key features:
  • Voter registration qualifications information
  • Voter registration lookup (to see if person is already registered)
  • Online voter registration application
